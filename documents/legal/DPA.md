# DATA PROCESSING AGREEMENT (DPA)

**Between:**

1. **NexuM Labs** ("Processor")
2. **[Client Name]** ("Controller")

## 1. Scope

This DPA applies to the processing of Personal Data by NexuM Labs on behalf of the Client as described in the MSA.

## 2. Processing of Personal Data

- **Subject Matter:** Software engineering and consulting services.
- **Duration:** Term of the MSA.
- **Nature and Purpose:** As reasonably necessary to provide the Services.
- **Categories of Data:** User contact information, system logs, application data.

## 3. Security Measures

Processor shall implement appropriate technical and organisational measures to ensure a level of security appropriate to the risk, including:

- Encryption of data in transit and at rest.
- Regular vulnerability assessments.
- Access controls and authentication.

## 4. Sub-processors

Client grants general authorization to Processor to engage sub-processors (e.g., AWS, Vercel, Supabase). Processor shall maintain a list of sub-processors.

## 5. Data Breaches

Processor shall notify Client without undue delay after becoming aware of a Personal Data Breach.
