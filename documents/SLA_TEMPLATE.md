# Service Level Agreement (SLA) - Enterprise Tier

**Effective Date:** January 1, 2026

## 1. Uptime Commitment

NexuM Labs guarantees a monthly uptime of **99.99%** for all Enterprise Tier services.

### Service Credits

| Monthly Uptime Percentage | Service Credit |
|---------------------------|----------------|
| < 99.99%                  | 10%            |
| < 99.9%                   | 25%            |
| < 99.0%                   | 50%            |

## 2. Incident Response Times

| Severity | Response Time (24/7) | Resolution Target |
|----------|----------------------|-------------------|
| Critical | 15 Minutes           | 4 Hours           |
| High     | 1 Hour               | 8 Hours           |
| Normal   | 4 Hours              | 2 Business Days   |
| Low      | 1 Business Day       | Next Release      |

## 3. Support Channels

- **Dedicated Slack Channel** for engineering teams.
- **24/7 Emergency Phone Line** for Critical incidents.
- **Email Support** for non-urgent inquiries.

## 4. Maintenance

Scheduled maintenance windows are communicated 7 days in advance and typically occur during off-peak hours (Sunday 02:00 UTC).
