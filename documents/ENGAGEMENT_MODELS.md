# NexuM Labs Engagement Models

## 1. Dedicated Engineering Team (Retainer)

**Best for:** Long-term product development, scaling existing platforms.

- **Structure:** Monthly retainer per engineer.
- **Commitment:** Min. 3 months.
- **Benefits:** Full integration into your Slack/Jira. Knowledge retention.

## 2. Strategic Consulting & Audit (Fixed)

**Best for:** Due diligence, architectural reviews, modernization roadmaps.

- **Structure:** Fixed fee per project phase.
- **Deliverables:** Technical Audit Report, Architecture Diagrams, Risk Assessment.

## 3. MVP Acceleration (Fixed Launch)

**Best for:** Early-stage startups moving from 0 to 1.

- **Structure:** Milestone-based.
- **Timeline:** typically 4-8 weeks.
- **Deliverables:** Production-ready MVP, Documentation, CI/CD Pipeline.
