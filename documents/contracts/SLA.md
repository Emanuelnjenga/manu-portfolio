# SERVICE LEVEL AGREEMENT (SLA)

**Tier:** Enterprise Retainer

## 1. Service Availability

NexuM Labs guarantees 99.9% availability for managed hosting services.

## 2. Support Response Times (SLA)

| Severity | Description | Response Time | Resolution Target |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Critical** | System down, data loss, blocking bug. | 1 hour (24/7) | 4 hours |
| **High** | Major feature broken, no workaround. | 4 hours (Bus. Hrs) | 1 business day |
| **Medium** | Minor issue, workaround available. | 1 business day | 3 business days |
| **Low** | Cosmetic issue, question. | 2 business days | Next Sprint |

## 3. Maintenance Windows

Scheduled maintenance will occur during off-peak hours (UTC weekends) with 48h notice.

## 4. Credits

If SLA is missed, Client is eligible for service credits of 5% of monthly fee per 1% below availability target, capped at 30%.
